Ingrediants for Chicken Curry:

  • Oil
  • Mustard
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Onion
  • tomato
  • Chicken
  • Coriander

Steps to prepare Chicken Curry:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the onion paste, garlic and ginger paste till golden brown.
  2. Add tomato puree.
  3. Add chicken pieces into the masala.
  4. Add 1/2 cup of water and stir.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander and serve.
